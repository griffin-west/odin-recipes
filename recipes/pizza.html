Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza with a crispy crust and melted cheese makes the perfect weeknight dinner or weekend project. The dough can be made ahead and refrigerated for up to 3 days.

Ingredients

  • 3½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Mix yeast with warm water and sugar. Let stand 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and oil. Knead 8-10 minutes.
  3. Let dough rise 1 hour until doubled.
  4. Divide dough in half. Roll each into 12-inch circle on floured surface.
  5. Top with sauce, cheese, and desired toppings.
  6. Bake at 450°F on preheated pizza stone or baking sheet for 12-15 minutes.
