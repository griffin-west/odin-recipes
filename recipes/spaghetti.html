Spaghetti

Description

Classic Italian spaghetti with meat sauce combines al dente pasta with a rich, garlicky tomato sauce. This version takes just 30 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (24 oz) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Brown beef, onion, and garlic. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add tomatoes and herbs. Simmer 20 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve sauce over pasta with grated parmesan.
