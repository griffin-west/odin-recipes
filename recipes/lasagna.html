Lasagna

Description

Classic homemade lasagna features layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and three melted cheeses for the ultimate Italian comfort food. This version uses simple ingredients but delivers restaurant-quality results.

Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced/li>
  • 2 (24 oz) jars marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown beef, onion, and garlic in large pot. Add marinara and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Mix ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, 1/2 cup parmesan, eggs, salt and pepper.
  4. Layer in 9x13 pan: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with remaining sauce and cheeses. Cover with foil.
  6. Bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake 25 minutes more until bubbly.
  7. Let rest 10-15 minutes before serving.
